Visitors App

Introduction

This app allows you to explore the trends revolving the total number of visitor arrivals from the world to Singapore for the period January 1978 to June 2015.  The data is downloadable from the data.gov.sg website and the codes to the app are uploaded to github.

 

Selection Controls

There are 3 types of selection controls and depending on the selections made, the two charts under the Chart tab will refresh accordingly. The charts show the aggregated number of visitors based on the parameters: region, country, year and month. More details can be found in the following table:

Controls Values & Results
Select by Period Type By Year:
The top chart shows the trend of visitors by year, color-coded by region.

By Month:
The top chart refreshes to show the trend of visitors by months of the year.

The selection does not affect the bottom chart.
Select by Year Range Both charts show aggregated number of visitors for the selected range.
Select by Region Both charts show aggregated number of visitors for the selected region(s).

 

 

Note:
The data on visitor arrivals are compiled from Disembarkation/ Embarkation cards completed by all visitors arriving in Singapore at the points of entry. It excludes (i)Malaysian citizens arriving by land;(ii)non-resident air and sea crew (except for sea crew flying in to join a ship);and (iii)air transit and transfer passengers
